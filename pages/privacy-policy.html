Peel - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Peel is an open-source Android app that turns websites into standalone, app-like experiences. It is powered by GeckoView (Mozilla's browser engine).

Peel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party. There are no analytics, tracking SDKs, or telemetry of any kind.

Web Content

Peel is a web browser. Websites you visit may collect data according to their own privacy policies. Peel provides privacy controls to help protect you, including:

  • Enhanced Tracking Protection
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal
  • Fingerprinting protection
  • Local network access blocking
  • WebRTC IP leak prevention
  • HTTPS-only mode

Data Storage

All browsing data (cookies, local storage, cache) is stored locally on your device. Peel supports per-app isolated sandboxes, meaning each web app can have its own separate cookies and storage. You can clear this data at any time.

No browsing data is sent to the developer or any external server.

Permissions

Peel requests the following Android permissions:

PermissionPurpose
InternetLoading web content (core functionality)
CameraWebsites requesting camera access (e.g. video calls, QR scanning)
MicrophoneWebsites requesting microphone access (e.g. voice messages, video calls)
Fine LocationWebsites requesting precise GPS location (e.g. maps)
Coarse LocationWebsites requesting approximate location
Storage (Android 12 and below)Saving downloaded files and uploading files to websites
NotificationsDownload progress and media playback notifications
Foreground ServiceBackground audio/video playback and file downloads
Audio SettingsMedia playback volume control
Wake LockPreventing device sleep during media playback
Install PackagesInstalling APK updates downloaded through the app

All permissions that provide access to sensitive data (camera, microphone, location) require explicit user consent at runtime. Peel itself does not use these permissions — they are passed through to websites that request them.

Third-Party Services

Peel does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party component is GeckoView, Mozilla's browser engine, which is used to render web content.

Children's Privacy

Peel is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
